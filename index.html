Stacker

Practice the arcade stacking game. Start the run, stop each moving row, keep the overlap, and choose whether to take the minor prize or continue for the major prize.

Ready to play

Press Start / Stack to begin.

The yellow blocks move left and right. Press Stack when they are directly above the red blocks below. Blocks that do not overlap are removed.

  • Start: The first row has three moving blocks.
  • Stack: Each stopped row must overlap the row below it.
  • Miss: If there is no overlap, the run ends on a red retry screen.
  • Minor prize: At the minor line, choose Take Minor Prize or Continue.
  • Major prize: Reach the top line to win the major prize.
